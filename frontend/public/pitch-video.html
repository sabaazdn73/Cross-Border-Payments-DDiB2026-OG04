Why We Anchor
Any edit changes the hash — tamper-evident by design
Verified independently on a public Mirror Node
No single party controls the record
Auditable trail without exposing personal data
Timestamped by network consensus, not our clock
Trust that doesn't rely on taking our word for it
Choosing the Rail
Fast finality — seconds, not minutes
Low, predictable fees
Publicly verifiable via open mirror nodes
Safe to prototype fully on public testnet first
Stablecoin liquidity already deep on-chain
Architecture leaves room to add other chains
Liquidity & Fallback
Liquidity depth checked before routing a transfer
Small transfers settle directly on the primary rail
Larger transfers route to deeper liquidity elsewhere
Slippage capped — never silently absorbed by the user
The routing decision itself gets hashed and anchored
Same guarantees, whichever rail is chosen
KYC & Compliance
Licensed exchange partners perform KYC / AML
We never custody raw identity documents
Only the compliance outcome is hashed, not the ID
One compliance record per transfer, anchored on-chain
Sender and receiver stay on regulated, licensed rails
