# Evaluation Axes

| Axis | Question | Our answer |
|---|---|---|
| Problem relevance | Is this real? | Cross-border friction is real; the compliance-evidence gap is the part nobody addresses |
| **Legality** | **Aren't you an unlicensed payment institution?** | **[this section](../legal/why-this-is-legal.md), no. We never hold funds. The licensed parties do. This is the agency model the EBA explicitly permits.** |
| Blockchain necessity | Why not a database? | The parties are independently licensed and mutually distrusting. A database owned by one of them cannot attest to another's compliance |
| Hedera necessity | Why Hedera? | [this section](../architecture/trust-layer.md), fixed USD fees, aBFT finality, Stablecoin Studio, and the consensus timestamp the trail depends on |
| Competition | Ramp, Bridge, Wise already exist | [this section](../business/market-analysis.md), they all leave the wallet in the flow, or they leave the trail unverifiable |
| Differentiation | Isn't "walletless" just custody? | Partly. That is why we say the licensed party custodies, not us |
| Coverage | You claim all countries? | **No. [this section](../legal/why-this-is-legal.md), that claim is false and we say why** |
| Capital efficiency | Zero lock-up? | **No. [this section](../business/business-model.md), we relocate it, we don't remove it** |
| Token | Where is the token? | [this section](../legal/why-this-is-legal.md), none, deliberately |
| Feasibility | Can you demo it? | [this section](demonstration-scenario.md), sandbox + testnet, end to end, live |
